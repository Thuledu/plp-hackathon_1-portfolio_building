Lemon Meringue Slice

Crust

1 x 200 g packet of tennis biscuits

¼ cup (60 ml) melted butter


Filling

2 x 385 g tins full cream sweetened condensed milk

½ cup (125 ml) boiling water

1 tablespoon (15 ml) grated lemon rind

½ cup (125 ml) freshly squeezed lemon juice

4 egg yolks

pinch of salt

2 teaspoons (10 ml) cornflour


Meringue layer

4 egg whites

2 teaspoons (10 ml) lemon juice

¼ cup (60 ml) castor sugar

¼ cup (60 ml) icing sugar


Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 180 °C.

2. Grease a 20 cm round cake tin and line the base with baking paper.

3. Crush the biscuits in a food processor until fine crumbs form.

4. Mix the melted butter with the biscuit crumbs and press into the base of the prepared tin.

5. Bake for 10 minutes, then remove from the oven and allow to cool.

6. For the filling, whisk together the condensed milk, boiling water, lemon rind, lemon juice, egg yolks, and salt until smooth.

7. Pour the filling over the cooled crust and bake for 15-20 minutes or until set.

8. For the meringue layer, beat the egg whites and lemon juice until soft peaks form.

9. Gradually add the castor sugar and icing sugar, beating until stiff peaks form.

10.Spread the meringue over the filling and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes or until golden brown.

11.Allow to cool completely before serving.
